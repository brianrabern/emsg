We are an interdisciplinary group of researchers at the University of Edinburgh who work on meaning, very broadly construed.

Researchers in the group are drawn from PPLS, which includes Philosophy, Psychology, and Linguistics, as well as from Informatics. Our concrete activities, include a biweekly talk series by external and internal speakers.

The group provides a venue where (a) scholars working on meanings across Edinburgh can regularly interact; and (b) early-career researchers are given opportunities to present their work to a broad academic audience and develop interdisciplinary connections.
